Main Point to Focus

  • Showcase Work: The main goal is to effectively showcase my projects, highlighting the skills and expertise I bring to the table. Each project should be presented in a way that's easy to understand for anyone visiting the website.
  • Provide Clarity: Create a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for visitors to navigate through the portfolio. A clear and simple structure ensures that people can quickly find the information they're looking for without any confusion.
  • Reflect Growth: Use the portfolio as a timeline to illustrate my professional journey. Include a section that demonstrates how my skills have evolved over time, showcasing a progression that reflects continuous learning and improvement.
  • Demonstrate Diversity: Ensure that the portfolio represents a diverse range of projects, emphasizing different skills and aspects of my work. This diversity provides a more comprehensive view of my capabilities and versatility.
  • Encourage Engagement: Incorporate features that invite interaction, such as a contact form or links to social media profiles. This encourages visitors to reach out for potential collaborations, job opportunities, or to simply engage in a conversation about the showcased work.
